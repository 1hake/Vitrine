Why so few options?

Because you don't need more. We picked the 3 best mockup layouts so you don't have to. Upload, title, export — that's it. No decision fatigue, no tweaking for hours.

Is it really free?

Yes. No account, no watermark, no limits. Your screenshots never leave your browser — everything runs locally.

What screenshot sizes does it export?

1290x2796 (6.7" — iPhone 15 Pro Max) and 1242x2688 (6.5" — iPhone 11 Pro Max). Both sizes required by Apple, both generated automatically.

Does it work for Google Play?

Yes. The exported resolutions work for both App Store and Google Play listings.

What formats can I upload?

PNG, JPG, or WebP. Exports are always high-quality PNG, zipped together when you have multiple mockups.
