Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Overview

Vitrine ("we", "our", "the tool") is a free online App Store mockup generator available at appstoremockup.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your screenshots and images are processed entirely in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers. All mockup generation happens client-side using JavaScript.
  • We do not require any account, login, or personal information to use the tool.
  • We do not store your projects, exported files, or any content you create.

Data We Collect

We use the following third-party services that may collect anonymous data:

  • Google AdSense — serves advertisements on our site. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

Cookies

Vitrine itself does not set any cookies. However, third-party advertising services (Google AdSense) may set cookies to serve relevant ads. These cookies are governed by Google's own privacy policy.

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to save corner calibration data for device templates. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites.

Children's Privacy

Vitrine does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The tool is intended for app developers and designers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us via our GitHub repository.
